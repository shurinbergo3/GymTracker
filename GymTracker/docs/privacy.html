Privacy Policy

App: Body Forge  |  Last updated: February 26, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Body Forge collects the following types of data to provide its core functionality:

  • Account information: Email address and display name (via Firebase Authentication or Google Sign-In).
  • Health & Fitness data: Workout sessions, sets, reps, body measurements, and weight records — stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your private cloud account.
  • HealthKit data: Heart rate, active calories, steps, and sleep data — read from Apple Health with your explicit permission. This data is used only within the app to display stats and is never shared with third parties.
  • Usage data: Anonymous crash reports and aggregate usage analytics (via Firebase) to help us improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve Body Forge features.
  • To sync your workout history and programs across your devices (Firebase Firestore).
  • To display personalized fitness analytics and progress charts.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or tracking purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework and optionally in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud).
  • Firebase Firestore is protected by security rules — only you can access your own data.
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

4. HealthKit

Body Forge requests access to Apple HealthKit to read heart rate, calories, steps, and sleep data, and to write completed workout sessions. This data is used exclusively within the app and is never transmitted to any server other than your own private Firebase account (linked to your login).

You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Body Forge.

5. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the app:

Settings → Account → Delete Account

This action will permanently remove your Firebase account, your Firestore data, and all locally stored data on the device.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, Firestore database, Crashlytics
  • Google Sign-In — Optional OAuth sign-in

7. Children's Privacy

Body Forge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via an in-app notice or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
